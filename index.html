EMI Calculator Guide

Our free EMI Calculator helps you calculate monthly loan repayments instantly for Home Loans, Car Loans, Personal Loans and other loans. Simply enter the loan amount, interest rate and tenure to view your monthly EMI, total interest and total repayment amount.

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount a borrower pays every month until the loan is fully repaid. Every EMI contains two components: Principal and Interest.

EMI Formula

EMI = P × R × (1+R)^N / ((1+R)^N − 1)

  • P = Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Loan Tenure in Months
